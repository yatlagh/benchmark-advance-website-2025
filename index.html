Instruction

Impactful, Research-Based Instructional Design

Aligned with the latest in Science of Reading research, Benchmark Advance takes an integrated approach to language arts instruction.

Purposeful Scope & Sequence

Research-based scope and sequence that moves students through a systematic progression of skills with spiral review to avoid decayed learning.

Explicit Instruction with Meaningful Practice

Effective daily instruction across word recognition and language comprehension skills provides opportunities for students to apply learning to real reading and writing experiences.

Built-In Differentiation

Whole-group and small-group instruction and materials support a wide range of learner needs for effective and efficient use of instructional time.

Build Knowledge

Build deep knowledge across grades through vertically aligned topics and essential questions. Students explore connected concepts, vocabulary, and text sets that develop comprehension, inquiry skills, and academic language — ensuring year-over-year growth and understanding.

Caregiver Support

Bridge Connections Between School and Home

Benchmark Advance enhances communication between school and home. Through Benchmark Universe, families have 24/7 access on any device to all learning resources and texts.

Family support includes:
  • Unit Welcome Letter – Available in multiple languages, these letters include fun, at-home activity suggestions that build vocabulary, comprehension, topic knowledge, and phonics and word study skills.
  • Unit Take-Home Activity Calendar – Each unit includes three weekly activities designed to reinforce prior learning at home.

Check out the Benchmark Advance Family Support website, available in English and Spanish.
